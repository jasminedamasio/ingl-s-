EXPLAINING... MONEY

discover the documentary



Explaining... Money: A Netflix documentary series that examines people's relationship with money and its many minefields.
Still considered taboo, the issue can cause anxiety, recklessness, avoidance, or even obsession. The phrase "Money in the
blink of an eye" says it all: the risk of being scammed by credit cards, the student loan crisis, the difficulty of saving enough
for old age, the excesses in casinos, and the scams that lead to wrong decisions. The production shows the weaknesses
that all human beings share when it comes to money, as well as the ways used to exploit it and the best strategies to avoid losses.






The series analyzes people's relationship with money and its many minefields. Among the topics covered are:



The risk of being scammed by credit cards: The series delves into the deceptive practices of credit card companies and how
they can trap consumers into debt. It highlights the importance of understanding credit card terms and conditions,
avoiding impulse purchases, and staying vigilant against fraudulent charges.


The student loan crisis: The series examines the growing burden of student loan debt and its impact on young adults'
financial futures. It explores the factors that have contributed to the crisis, such as rising tuition costs and predatory
lending practices. It also discusses potential solutions, such as student loan forgiveness programs and income-driven repayment plans.


The difficulty of saving money: The series acknowledges the challenges individuals face in saving for the future,
particularly in today's economy. It offers practical tips and strategies for overcoming these obstacles, such as creating a budget,
setting realistic goals, and automating savings contributions.


The excesses of casinos: The series exposes the allure and dangers of gambling, particularly in the context of casinos.
It highlights the addictive nature of gambling and the devastating financial consequences it can have on individuals and families.
It encourages viewers to be aware of the risks and to exercise caution when engaging in any form of gambling.


The scams that lead to bad decisions: The series warns against the prevalence of scams and their ability to manipulate
people's emotions and judgment. It provides tips on how to identify and avoid scams, such as being wary of unsolicited offers,
verifying information before acting, and never rushing into a decision.


Overall, the series aims to educate viewers about the complexities of personal finance and empower them to make informed
financial decisions. It encourages them to be proactive in protecting their financial well-being and to avoid falling prey
to common pitfalls.






Explaining... Money: A Summary of each episode



Episode 1: Credit Cards: An American Addiction

Immerse yourself in the addictive world of easy credit and the psychological traps that lead us to spend beyond our means.
Discover how credit card companies exploit our desires and vulnerabilities to keep us in debt.
Learn strategies to get rid of credit card debt and take back control of your finances.


Episode 2: Cryptocurrencies: A Bubble or the Future of Money?
Explore the fascinating and volatile world of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.
Understand the technology behind cryptocurrencies and their impacts on the global economy.
Discover the risks and potential rewards of investing in cryptocurrencies, and whether it's the right option for you.


Episode 3: The Dark Side of the Stock Market
Uncover the secrets of the stock market and the strategies used by great investors to make a profit.
Discover the cases of market manipulation and fraud that shook investor confidence.
Learn how to invest safely in the stock market and make informed financial decisions.


Episode 4: Sports Betting: A Risky Game
Explore the multi-billion dollar sports betting industry and the dangers of gaming addiction.
Discover how bookmakers operate and the techniques they use to attract and retain bettors.
Learn how to protect yourself from the risks of sports betting and bet responsibly.


Episode 5: Student Finance: An Impossible Debt
Address the student loan crisis in the United States and the devastating impact debt has on young people's lives.
Discover the stories of people struggling to pay their student loans and the social consequences of this problem.
Explore potential solutions to the student finance crisis and how to ensure access to higher education for all.


Episode 6: Retirement: An Uncertain Future
Face the challenges of retirement in an ever-changing world and the importance of planning for your financial future.
Discover the different pension systems available and how to choose the best one for you.
Learn how to invest for retirement and achieve your long-term financial goals.
